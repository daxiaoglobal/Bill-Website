Bill Coedy

Featured Artist

A passionate lover of the Kootenay region's pristine water streams and mountain landscapes. Bill's work captures the raw beauty and spiritual essence of British Columbia's wilderness, translating the movement of rivers and the stillness of alpine peaks into vivid, expressive compositions.

This piece explores the relationship between form and formlessness, capturing a moment of transformation through layered textures and carefully considered color harmonies. Created using a combination of traditional techniques and contemporary approaches.
